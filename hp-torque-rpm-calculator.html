HP Torque RPM Calculator

Calculate torque from horsepower and RPM or horsepower from torque. This calculator is commonly used for electric motors, engines, conveyors, robotics, pumps, and mechanical power transmission systems.


Key Mechanical Power Formula

Torque (lb-ft) = (HP × 5252) / RPM

HP = (Torque × RPM) / 5252

The constant 5252 comes from converting rotational power into horsepower units.
