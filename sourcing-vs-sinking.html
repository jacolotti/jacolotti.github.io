Sourcing vs Sinking: Why Your PLC Inputs or Outputs Aren’t Working

If your sensor is on but the PLC input never changes — or your output turns on in logic but the device does nothing — this is usually a sourcing vs sinking mismatch.

Quick Explanation (What Actually Matters)

Sourcing

Sends +24V to the device (PNP)

Sinking

Sends path to 0V/common (NPN)

Rule: sourcing connects to sinking
