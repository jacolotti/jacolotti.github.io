Beam Deflection Calculator

Quickly estimate deflection for cantilever and simply supported beams using real engineering inputs. Ideal for machine frames, brackets, tooling, and structural supports.

Cantilever: δ = (F × L³) / (3 × E × I)
Simply Supported: δ = (F × L³) / (48 × E × I)
