Upload Your Photo

Upload a machine or component photo. The tool will classify the image and recommend the closest matching section of AutomationCalculators.net.

Tip: Add a few words if the image is confusing. Examples: “weld electrode,” “PLC panel,” “air cylinder,” “robot gripper,” or “conveyor motor.”

Why the hint helps

Some industrial parts look similar in photos. A weld electrode, motor shaft, pneumatic rod, and tooling pin can all look cylindrical. A short hint helps the classifier prioritize function instead of shape.

Low-confidence results

If the tool is not confident, it shows two likely categories instead of forcing you into the wrong page. That is better for plant-floor troubleshooting where photos can be dark, cropped, or taken at odd angles.

Feedback improves routing

After a result appears, mark whether the classification was useful. Those feedback events help identify which categories need better rules.
