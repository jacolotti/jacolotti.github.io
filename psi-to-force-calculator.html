PSI to Force Calculator

Use this PSI to force calculator to convert pressure and diameter into force using piston area. This is useful for pneumatic cylinders, hydraulic cylinders, presses, clamps, tooling, and general pressure-to-force calculations.

This calculator estimates theoretical force from pressure and area. Real-world output force can be lower due to friction, seal drag, pressure loss, rod area, and mechanical inefficiencies.

Pressure to Force Calculator

Force = Pressure × Area
Area = π × Diameter² / 4
