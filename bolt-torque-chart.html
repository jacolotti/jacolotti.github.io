Bolt Torque Calculator

Estimate tightening torque using a simplified engineering formula.

Torque = K × F × D

Where:
K = friction factor (~0.18–0.22 typical)
F = clamp load (N)
D = bolt diameter (m)

Actual torque requirements depend on lubrication, thread condition, joint stiffness, and bolt grade.
