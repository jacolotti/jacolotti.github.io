Labor Savings Calculator

Estimate annual labor savings from automation based on time reduction, labor cost, and operating schedule. This is one of the core drivers behind automation ROI.

What this calculator does

This calculates how much labor cost is removed or repurposed when a process becomes more efficient. It converts time saved into real annual dollars.

Formula:

Labor Savings = Hours Saved × Labor Rate × Shifts × Days

Engineering notes

  • Use fully burdened labor rate (not base wage)
  • Do not double count labor already eliminated elsewhere
  • Validate that saved time is actually recoverable
  • Consider redeployment instead of elimination
